About Us

We are a team of open source contributors who have created this website Mind Mood, which is a mental health platform That uses AI to detect emotional states through facial expressions. Our goal is to help identify early signs of stress, anxiety, or depression in a simple, non-invasive way.

By combining technology with care, we aim to support mental well-being and emotional awareness for everyone. stress free life with the help of MindMood
